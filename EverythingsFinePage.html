    FINE

    "Everything’s Fine" is a 2D side scroller game inspired by the classic Mario and Kirby games. While the presentation pays homage to the graphics of these games, the subject matter relates deeply to themes of Night in the Woods. The player will be exploring a specific experience of anxiety, called atychiphobia. Chi is the main playable character, and they’re a college student struggling through their daily life as they suffer from atychiphobia, the fear of failure. Will Chi be able to make it through their week, while overcoming their daily obstacles? Unlike traditional Mario platformers, Everything’s fine attempts to subvert player’s expectations through constant triggers and obstacles that re-imagine traditional Mario style platformers. Everything's fine asks players to utilize both narrative and mechanics to help Chi from their worst enemy… themselves.
