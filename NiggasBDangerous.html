    • NIGGAS B

    DANGEROUS

    "Niggas B Dangerous" is a rotoscope tribute to the Black Power movement originating from the Black Pather Party. The Black Panther Party was a revolutionary political orginization that was created to act as defense and protection for Black people in response to the police brutality war in the streets. Like the time of the BPP, the country has felt the effects of police brutality again. We are in the time of the Black Lives Matter Movement. We are still yelling Black Lives Matter. "Niggas B Dangerous" is a call to Black people to wake up. They kill us when we're unarmed, driving, selling CDs, and playing with waterguns. Black peope are viewed as a threat when all we're trying to do is live. The rotoscope takes place in a library because knowledge is power. We must continue to stand up, stand out, and educate.
