    • MOLLY

    "MOLLY" is a split screen, “Call and Response”, short film. The short film discusses the issues of sexual assault and domestic violence while showing two different sides of her experience in the world. The left is her external and the right is her internal. The poem is based off an accumulation of experiences and stories of both Arabia and those told to her by friends and family members. The two videos displaying side by side symbolizes the way that she and other suvivors move through the world. She wanted to show the contrast of what people see and what people don’t see.
