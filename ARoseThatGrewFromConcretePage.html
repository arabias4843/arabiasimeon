    "A Rose That Grew From Concrete" is a rotoscope that explores and illustrates the coming of age story of Arabia. She adopts the title "A Rose That Grew From Concrete", from Tupac's book of poems that she first read when she was in middle school. Growing up in the "hood" was a journey that was extremely hard at times. "A Rose That Grew From Concrete" acts as a moment of reflection and appreciation for all of the hard work and sacrafice.
